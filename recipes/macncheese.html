Mac N Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a staple dish for both children and adults as it is easy to make and cheap as well. It can be enjoyed as a side or as a main dish with meat and other ingredients added. Truly a spectacular dish.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Margarine Butter
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Drain Water
  3. Stir in Milk, Butter, and Cheese
