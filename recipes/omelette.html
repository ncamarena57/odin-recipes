Omelette

Description

An omelette is a simple but delicious breakfast meal that can whipped up in less than ten minutes. Don't know what to eat for breakfast but don't have a lot of time to spare? Make an omelette!

Ingredients

  • Two Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Meat
  • Veggies
  • Cooking Oil
  1. Whisk eggs, add seasoning
  2. Pre-heat pan, add oil
  3. Fry in pan
  4. Add stuffing, turn over
