Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is one of the most popular desserts around! This sweet treat is perfect for birthday parties, holidays, or really any event!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Baking Powder
  • Frosting
  • Cake Mix
  • Sugar
  1. Mix eggs, butter, milk, baking powder, sugar, and cake mix into bowl
  2. Pre-heat oven
  3. Bake in oven
  4. Add frosting
